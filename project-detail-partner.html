As Time Goes By

Feature Film · Period Drama · Music & Culture · Set in Chicago (1950s–2000s)

Project overview

A sweeping, music-driven drama about love, legacy, and the sound of a changing city.

Format: Feature Film

Genre: Period Drama · Romantic Elements

Tone: Intimate, emotional, hopeful

Primary audience: Adult drama audiences; fans of music-driven stories

Logline

A gifted DJ fights to protect his sound, his city, and the love of his life as Chicago transforms around him across five decades.

World & story snapshot

Set across 1950, 1955, 1962, and 2005 Chicago—with key beats during the Vietnam War—As Time Goes By charts the evolution of Black music, radio, and community through one man’s fight to keep his voice on the air.

  • Multi-period Chicago
  • Civil rights era backdrop
  • Music as connective tissue

Positioning & audience

This project is positioned for streamers and distributors seeking grounded, character-driven stories anchored in real-world history, with a strong Black lead and a built-in music audience.

  • Awards-leaning drama
  • Music and nostalgia appeal
  • Cross-generational Black audiences
